An unconventional path — on purpose.

Interdisciplinary curiosity has always been my driving force. I spent six years running a federally funded clinical research program at SMU, investigating how biobehavioral drivers influence subconscious choices. My background in behavioral research includes two peer-reviewed publications, which solidified my ability to translate dense data into actionable, human-centric strategies. That experience taught me how to navigate high-stakes data, manage complex stakeholder ecosystems, and find the non-linear correlations that others miss.

Eventually, I moved from the lab to the digital world to apply that same rigor to Experience Operations.

Today, I specialize in the "behind-the-scenes" architecture of CX. I map journeys and design the workflows, content pipelines, and templates that allow teams to deliver seamless experiences at scale. Across projects, I tend to gravitate toward the same core question:

Why isn't this system working as well as it should — and how do we fix it without losing the human element?
