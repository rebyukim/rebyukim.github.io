Background Skills

An unconventional path — on purpose.

I spent six years running a federally-funded clinical research program at SMU before stepping into marketing and creative strategy. It taught me how to manage complex ecosystems, speak the language of diverse stakeholders, and—most importantly—how to stay calm when the stakes are high and the data is dense. That experience shaped how I approach experience design: understanding people first, then building systems that support them.

My work focuses on improving how organizations operate behind the scenes — mapping journeys, aligning stakeholders, and designing the workflows, templates, and content pipelines that allow teams to deliver better experiences consistently.

Across projects, I tend to gravitate toward the same core question:

Why isn't this system working as well as it should — and how do we fix it without losing the human element?
