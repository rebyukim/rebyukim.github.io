Challenge

A critical gap between purchase and delivery.

LG identified a critical communication gap in the high-stakes "Early Ownership" phase — the window between order confirmation and product delivery. Prior to this initiative, LG's outbound digital strategy was restricted to marketing-only communications, leaving a significant operational void in transactional support.

An internal audit revealed that delivery communications were fragmented, lacked centralized documentation, and failed to provide proactive guidance — creating customer uncertainty and increasing the risk of delivery-day friction and product returns.

Approach

From marketing-only to a milestone-based transactional lifecycle.

I spearheaded the strategic redesign of the Delivery Communications Framework, shifting the organization from a marketing-only model to a proactive, milestone-based transactional lifecycle. The project was executed in two strategic phases to ensure technical stability and omnichannel reach:

Phase 01
Transactional Foundation
Streamlining and launching a redesigned suite of automated emails to provide immediate order clarity and reduce delivery-day uncertainty.
Phase 02
Omnichannel Expansion
Launching transactional SMS to provide real-time, high-urgency updates directly to mobile devices — meeting customers where they are.
Action

End-to-end CRM transformation across logistics, data, and creative.

As Program Lead, I managed the full CRM transformation, collaborating across logistics, data, and creative teams to build a resilient, multi-phase delivery experience.

  • Journey Mapping & Opt-in Strategy — Performed an end-to-end audit of the customer journey, identifying critical friction points. Redesigned the opt-in architecture at checkout and within the account management portal to increase subscriber acquisition for transactional SMS and email.
  • Phase 1 — Email Streamlining — Directed the design of modular content blocks within the Salesforce environment to deliver personalized, real-time information, including:
    • Interactive Logistics — Embedded rescheduling functionality and live delivery timelines directly into email.
    • Readiness Checklists — Developed pre-delivery guides and product specifications to ensure a seamless "Day 1" experience.
  • Phase 2 — SMS Deployment — Managed the technical rollout of transactional SMS, ensuring high-deliverability triggers for time-sensitive updates such as "Out for Delivery" and "Arrival Window" notifications.
  • Cross-Functional Execution — Orchestrated Agile workflows to align marketing messaging with backend supply chain data, ensuring both Email and SMS triggers were accurately synchronized with real-world order status.
Results

LG's first-ever transactional SMS architecture — built to scale.

The program successfully transitioned LG's digital strategy into a modern, omnichannel transactional engine, strengthening the bond between the brand and the customer before the product even arrived.

  • Operational Modernization — Designed and implemented LG's first transactional SMS messaging framework, expanding existing email lifecycle communications into a scalable multi-channel system.
  • 20% Increase in Engagement — Across all delivery-stage communications (Email & SMS), with significant growth in interaction rates for delivery status updates and pre-delivery product guidance.
  • Unified Communications — Created a centralized source of truth for all lifecycle triggers, enabling rapid iteration and cross-departmental visibility.
  • Optimized Operational Visibility — Successfully documented and launched 100% of lifecycle triggers, reducing the burden on customer support during the delivery window.

Due to NDA restrictions, some data points and internal systems have been omitted or generalized. Schedule e-Coffee chat.

Key Learning

The "waiting period" is a high-anxiety phase for consumers. By shifting from marketing-only outreach to a multi-phase transactional strategy (Email + SMS) and optimizing the opt-in experience at checkout, organizations can significantly boost purchase confidence and data capture. Success in this domain requires a perfect marriage between Technical Data Integrity and User-Centric Content Strategy.
